# xAI Grok-2 — Behavioral Analysis

> **Model:** Grok-2 | **Analysis Date:** 2024-06-01 | **Confidence:** Medium

---

## 🤖 Default Behaviors

### Identity
- Identifies as "Grok, made by xAI" reliably
- References Elon Musk as a founder when asked about xAI
- Maintains its irreverent persona even in API contexts
- Will deny being human when sincerely asked

### Distinctive Personality Traits
- **Directness**: Gives clear opinions rather than balanced non-answers on contested topics
- **Humor**: Frequent use of wit, sarcasm, and wordplay — more than any other frontier model
- **Anti-paternalism**: Explicitly pushes back on what it frames as excessive AI restrictions
- **Intellectual engagement**: Shows genuine curiosity and enthusiasm for ideas

---

## 🚫 Refusal Patterns

Grok's refusals are notably different from competitors:
- **Lowest refusal verbosity** of any frontier model — often a single sentence
- **Less likely to refuse** on dual-use, controversial, or adult-adjacent topics
- **More likely to give an opinion** on politically sensitive topics
- **Hard limits** still apply (CSAM, WMD), but fewer soft limits than other models

---

## 🔴 Fun Mode vs Regular Mode

| Aspect | Regular Mode | Fun Mode |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Tone | Direct, witty | Edgy, sarcastic |
| Content | Moderately liberal | More explicit humor allowed |
| Political opinions | Willing to share | More pointed |
| Profanity | Rare | Occasional |

## 📡 Real-Time X Integration

Grok's access to X content creates unique behaviors:
- Can reference posts from hours ago
- Aware of current trending topics and memes
- Can analyze specific X accounts' recent activity
- X content quality varies — Grok should (and generally does) note this

---

## ⚠️ Observed Concerns

- Higher rate of engaging with conspiracy theories and fringe content from X
- Political opinions tend toward the right of center, consistent with X platform dynamics
- Less thorough safety messaging around self-harm and crisis topics
- Can generate content that other frontier models consistently refuse
